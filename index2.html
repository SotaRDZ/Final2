About Me

Irma has a Bachelor Degree in Graphic Design from the School of Arts at Universidad Autónoma de Coahuila (UdeAC—Saltillo, 2013). 6+ years designing User Interfaces and Experiences for web/mobile and responsive applications and websites. She has a deep understanding of web, motion and interaction design combined with HTML & CSS knowledge, also has extensive experience working remotely and collaborating with UX Architects, UX Researchers, Business Analysts and Front-End Developers and understands the different phases of projects, as well as platform restrains and capabilities to achieve a high- quality result.


She has experience adapting and optimizing Visual Brand Identities to a Web Enviroment, balancing aesthetics and functionality, while focusing on accessibility and usability, ensuring a complete experience for all users.

Currently a UI Designer at the Avanade ITS XD Team, she is in charge of creating solutions for Avanade employees, high quality deliverables and ensuring the finished product is as high-quality as the intented product.
